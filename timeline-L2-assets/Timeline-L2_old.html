Production years and release cadence

Two concentrated production blitzes (2028, 2030) manufacture scale efficiently, followed by paced releases that keep the saga present over five consecutive years. Two-part films release in spring/summer, while the series follows each fall—creating a steady annual rhythm.

Key locations

The four key anchor locations for the family and the audience

In a large saga, repetition is not redundancy: it is reassurance. Returning to the same four family locations creates a familiar rhythm for viewers, so each new conflict feels grounded inside a stable world.

These anchors also help production scale intelligently: sets, costumes, and visual signatures can be reused and evolved rather than rebuilt from scratch, keeping quality consistent across films and seasons.
