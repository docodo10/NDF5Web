At a glance
  • A prestige crime-family saga built on duality: conscience vs ambition.
  • Five annual chapters set in the 2000s, spanning continents and generations.
  • An intimate triangle of power, desire, secrets: Domenico, Lenny, Greta.

Learn More

Through the deconstruction of the criminal mind, the author crafts a spectacular and beautifully rendered psychological crime saga, The New Drago Family.

Masters of finance and moral ambiguity, the very wealthy Dragos stand at the heart of an extravagant triangle: the charismatic Don Domenico Drago, striving to protect his family identity and empire; Lenny, the ambitious counselor whose intellect outruns his conscience; and Greta, the wise navigator steering between art, love, and danger.

Across a multidimensional storyline laced with secrets, shocking twists, and unexpected revelations, two renowned directors express the opposing states of the criminal mind—driving a five-year odyssey, an annual chronology set in the 2000s—from the Drago world to the ultimate summit of American society—where conscience yields to greed and ambition in the corroded pursuit of the American dream.
