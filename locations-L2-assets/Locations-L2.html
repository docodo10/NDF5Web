Signature Properties

Four environments. One recognizable world.

The Drago Island, Dimansion, The Grande Paolo, and New York Drago Castle are recurring dramatic engines. Dimansion and TGP sit within the larger Drago Estate in Santa Barbara.

TDI The Estate Dimansion TGP NYDC

TDI · Dominica

The Drago Island

The annual Caribbean gathering: elite networking, pleasure, secrecy, surveillance, political influence, deals, contraband exchanges, and danger concealed beneath paradise.

Santa Barbara

The Drago Estate

The umbrella environment connecting Dimansion, The Grande Paolo, forests, gardens, private roads, security areas, and the Family’s emotional and operational life.

The emotional home

Dimansion

The domestic center of the saga: romance, breakfasts, family dinners, Christmas celebrations, private recovery, staff life, surveillance, secrets, and internal conflict.

The command center

The Grande Paolo

The strategic brain of the Estate, where the Family manages finance, law, crisis, ethical debate, retaliation, intelligence, and succession pressure.

NYDC · New York

New York Drago Castle

The Family’s New York fortress and old-world power base, connecting criminal authority, politics, law-enforcement pressure, private vaults, image, and confrontation.

Recurring Story Territories

An international dramatic geography

Six territories recur across the saga, each carrying its own atmosphere, history, alliances, and pressure.

Dominica / The Drago Island

Paradise, sovereignty, local alliances, secrecy, pleasure, danger, and maritime transfer operations.

Santa Barbara

The Family’s emotional and operational home: estate life, beaches, art, medicine, hospitality, and relationships.

Montréal

Family history, culture, celebration, recruitment, transition, and the production hub behind the project.

Milan

Art, opera, fashion, business, romance, sophistication, heritage, and violence.

Trapani / Sicily

Heritage, exile, family origins, protection, romance, identity, and rediscovery.

New York

The urban battlefield: NYDC, finance, law enforcement, political access, and underworld control.

Designed for an intelligent hybrid production

The target environments can combine practical recurring rooms, partial façades, modular and redressable interiors, LED or virtual production, digital set extensions, CGI/VFX, and selected location photography.

Modular interiors

Redressable architecture that supports multiple story functions without flattening identity.

Digital extensions

Virtual production and VFX extend scale, geography, and controlled visual ambition.
